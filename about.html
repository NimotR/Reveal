About Us

Who we are

The PTWTTW project is a Muslim oriented team project which is led by Ogundipe Abdullateef (founder and team leader). This is a project primary to focus and help a current problem faced by our society with respect to mental health. The PTWTTW project is a Muslim oriented team project which is led by Ogundipe Abdullateef (founder and team leader). This is a project primary to focus and help a current problem faced by our society with respect to mental health.

The thought of PTWTTW project is to show that the youth are not too young to make a difference in the society, make positive impacts in the lives of people around them, locality, cities, states, countries and the world.

PTWTTW team believe that we, youth, are product of the society and as such, we should find ways to give back to the society in tackling crucial problem in way of return. This is the whole reason why we exist.

PTWTTW is a project for everyone irrespective of our difference, say; age group, religion, tribe, culture, languages, etc. The mental health problem is a problem faced by all, most especially the youth who are the future of the country.

Negative mind, loss hope, lack of confidence, unmotivated, lack of vision, priorities misconception, dwelling too much on past, suicide and all, have been the problem of youth hindering their development and integration in the society.

This need to change, most especially, in our country, if really want to grow. Aside the youth, other age group are affected and this has negative impacts on the nation as a whole.
